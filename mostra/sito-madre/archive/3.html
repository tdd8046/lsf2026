AntiDisciplinary Lab
We are used to assigning value to photos of family or the people we share our homes, lives.
People at different times, alone, together, always during a movement, an action. These entities are the smart sensors populating our homes. Privacy and personal data are certainly key. However, other entities have now joined in that actively and autonomously take photographs of these same.
